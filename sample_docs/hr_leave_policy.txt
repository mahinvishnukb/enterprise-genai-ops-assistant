Enterprise Operations Inc. — HR Leave Policy

All full-time employees accrue 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar
year, credited at the start of each quarter. Unused leave up to 5 days may
be carried over into the next calendar year; any remaining balance beyond
that is forfeited on December 31st.

Sick leave is separate from annual leave: employees receive 10 paid sick
days per year, no carryover. A medical certificate is required for sick
leave longer than 3 consecutive days.

Onboarding steps for new employees:
1. Complete HR intake forms within the first 3 business days.
2. Set up payroll and benefits enrollment within the first 2 weeks.
3. Complete mandatory compliance and security training within 30 days.
4. Meet with your manager to set 90-day goals.

Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 5 business
days in advance for planned leave. Emergency leave should be reported to
your manager as soon as possible, with formal documentation submitted
within 48 hours.
