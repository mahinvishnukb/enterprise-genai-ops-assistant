Q1 Operations Report — Summary

Shipment volume grew 12% quarter-over-quarter, driven by increased demand
on the Toronto-Vancouver and Calgary-Montreal corridors. On-time delivery
rate held at 86%, slightly below the 90% target.

Key risk: port congestion in Vancouver added an average of 2.3 days of
delay to westbound shipments in February and March. Mitigation in
progress: rerouting a portion of westbound volume through Seattle.

Cancellation rate remained low at 4% of total shipments, consistent with
Q4. No material change in carrier performance was observed.

Recommended action items for Q2: renegotiate SLA terms with the Vancouver
port operator, and pilot a secondary carrier on the Calgary-Montreal route
to reduce single-carrier dependency.
