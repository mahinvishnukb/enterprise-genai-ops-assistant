Propgatics Logistics Intelligence Platform — Q1 2026 Operations Report

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Q1 2026 shipment volume reached 24,823 across all Propgatics routes — a 12% increase quarter-over-quarter, driven by demand growth on the Toronto–Vancouver and Calgary–Montreal corridors. On-time delivery rate: 75.3% (industry benchmark: 85%). Total shipping revenue: CAD $1.84M. Total delivery cost: CAD $4.68M. Cost-to-revenue ratio remains elevated at 2.5x, driven by carrier operating costs — a pricing review has been initiated.

KEY RISK: WEATHER IMPACT ON VANCOUVER-BOUND ROUTES
Severe winter weather on mountain pass routes added an average of 3.1 hours of delay to Vancouver-bound long-haul shipments in February and March. Mitigation: weather-aware routing algorithm under evaluation. All five carriers (UPS, FedEx, DHL, Canada Post, Purolator) affected equally — confirms systemic route-level rather than carrier-level cause.

1. Implement weather-aware routing for Vancouver-bound long-haul shipments — target 5pp reduction in delay rate by Q2 end.
2. Initiate pricing model review — delivery cost at 2.5x revenue is unsustainable; target cost-to-revenue ratio of 1.8x by year-end.
3. Renegotiate SLA terms with FedEx (lowest on-time rate at 74.96%) — minimum 76% on-time target for Q2.

5. Reduce Critical severity incident rate from 10% to 7% through proactive driver check-in protocol on high-risk routes.
6. Target overall on-time delivery rate of 78%+ by Q2 end through route optimization and weather-aware dispatch.
